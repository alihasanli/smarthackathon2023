Smart Karabakh hackathon launched within TEKNOFEST Azerbaijan

Smart Karabakh hackathon is being held as part of the TEKNOFEST Azerbaijan Aerospace and Technology Festival which will be held for the first time in Baku on 26–29 May this year, jointly organized by the Turkish Technology Team Foundation (T3 Foundation), Ministry of Digital Development and Transport of the Republic of Azerbaijan and Ministry of Industry and Technology of the Republic of Turkey. Thirty teams from Azerbaijan and Turkey will win the right to compete in the final and offer smart solutions for the transformation of the liberated Karabakh into a normal region.
